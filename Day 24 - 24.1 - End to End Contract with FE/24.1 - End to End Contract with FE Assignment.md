## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ **Not checking wallet connection**: Always verify before showing UI
- ❌ **Not formatting BigInt**: Divide by 1e18 for ETH display
- ❌ **Missing providers**: Wrap app in both Wagmi and Query providers
- ❌ **Hardcoded addresses**: Use environment variables or constants
- ❌ **No error handling**: Always try/catch or handle hook errors
- ❌ **Not waiting for confirmations**: Use useWaitForTransactionReceipt
- ❌ **Forgetting `value` field**: stake() needs both args and value
- ❌ **Wrong ABI**: Must match deployed contract exactly
