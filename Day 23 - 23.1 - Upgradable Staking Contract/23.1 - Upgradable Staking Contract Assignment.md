## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ **Not claiming rewards before stake update**: Leads to reward loss
- ❌ **Storage layout mismatch**: Corrupts proxy storage
- ❌ **Integer overflow in rewards**: Use safe math or Solidity 0.8+
- ❌ **Not testing time-based logic**: Use vm.warp extensively
- ❌ **Forgetting fallback in proxy**: Calls won't forward
- ❌ **Not handling zero stakes**: Division by zero errors
- ❌ **Missing access control**: Anyone can upgrade/mint
- ❌ **Not testing with real addresses**: Use vm.prank
