## 💼 Career Paths in Web3

### 1. **Smart Contract Developer**
**Focus**: Solidity, Anchor, Security
- **Projects**: Build a DEX, Lending protocol, NFT marketplace
- **Skills Needed**: Deep contract knowledge, testing, auditing
- **Salary Range**: $80k - $200k+
- **Companies**: Uniswap, Aave, Compound, MakerDAO

### 2. **Full-Stack Web3 Developer**
**Focus**: Frontend + Contracts + Backend
- **Projects**: Complete dApps with beautiful UIs
- **Skills Needed**: React, TypeScript, Smart contracts, Design
- **Salary Range**: $70k - $180k+
- **Companies**: OpenSea, Foundation, Zora

### 3. **Trading Bot Developer**
**Focus**: Automation, MEV, Arbitrage
- **Projects**: Advanced trading bots, MEV bots, Snipers
- **Skills Needed**: Trading strategies, Fast execution, Math
- **Salary Range**: Unlimited (if profitable)
- **Path**: Independent or join trading firms

### 4. **Blockchain Infrastructure Engineer**
**Focus**: Nodes, Indexers, RPCs
- **Projects**: Custom RPC nodes, Blockchain explorers, Data APIs
- **Skills Needed**: Go, Rust, Distributed systems
- **Salary Range**: $100k - $250k+
- **Companies**: Alchemy, QuickNode, Helius, The Graph

### 5. **Security Auditor**
**Focus**: Finding vulnerabilities, Protecting protocols
- **Projects**: Audit reports, Bug bounties, Security tools
- **Skills Needed**: Deep security knowledge, Attack vectors
- **Salary Range**: $120k - $300k+ (or bounties)
- **Companies**: Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin, ConsenSys Diligence

### 6. **DeFi Protocol Designer**
**Focus**: Tokenomics, Game theory, Economics
- **Projects**: Design new protocols, Economic models
- **Skills Needed**: Finance, Math, Game theory
- **Salary Range**: $90k - $220k+
- **Companies**: Curve, Balancer, Yearn

## 💡 Words of Wisdom

### From Successful Web3 Developers:

**"The best way to learn is to build."** 
- Don't wait for perfection. Ship projects, get feedback, iterate.

**"Focus on solving real problems."**
- Don't build for your resume. Build what users need.

**"Community is everything in Web3."**
- Help others, contribute to open source, share your knowledge.

**"Specialize, but stay broad enough."**
- Be known for something, but don't limit yourself.

**"The pace of change is insane."**
- What you learned today might be outdated in 6 months. Stay curious.
