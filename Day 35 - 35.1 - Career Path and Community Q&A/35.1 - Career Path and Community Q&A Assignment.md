## Core Assignment — Your Web3 Career Roadmap

This is **NOT a coding assignment**. This is about **planning your future** in Web3 and engaging with the community to get real-world guidance.

## Task 1 — Self-Assessment & Reflection

Write honest answers to these questions in a document:

### Technical Interests

1. **Which topics from the 34 days excited you the most?**
   - List your top 3 favorite topics
   - Why did they interest you?
   - What would you like to learn more about?

2. **Which projects did you enjoy building the most?**
   - Which made you lose track of time?
   - Which felt like "work" vs "fun"?
   - What does this tell you about your interests?

3. **What's your learning style?**
   - Do you prefer building products or solving hard problems?
   - Do you like exploring new things or mastering one thing deeply?
   - Do you enjoy frontend, backend, or smart contracts most?

### Career Goals

4. **What's your timeline?**
   - Need a job ASAP (3 months)?
   - Can invest in deep learning (6-12 months)?
   - Want to build a product/startup?

5. **What's your financial situation?**
   - Need income now?
   - Can afford to learn and build?
   - Looking for equity/upside?

6. **What's your risk tolerance?**
   - Prefer stable salary or high-risk/high-reward?
   - Interested in startups or established companies?
   - Want to build your own product?

### Skills Assessment

7. **Rate yourself honestly (1-10):**
   - Smart contract development: ___/10
   - Frontend development: ___/10
   - Backend development: ___/10
   - Security/Auditing: ___/10
   - DeFi understanding: ___/10
   - Trading/MEV: ___/10
   - System design: ___/10

8. **What are your biggest skill gaps?**
   - What do you struggle with?
   - What would make you more hireable?
   - What skills do you see in job postings you don't have?

## Community Response Examples

### Good Community Post Example ✅
```
Hey everyone! 👋

Just wrapped up the 100xDevs Web3 track and I'm blown away 
by how much I've learned. From not knowing what a private key 
was to building a full trading bot with Jupiter integration!

My biggest takeaway: Building is the best teacher. The trading 
bot project (Days 33-34) taught me more than weeks of theory.

Now I'm torn between:
1. Going deep on security (auditing really interests me)
2. Building a SaaS product for Web3 creators

For those who've been in my shoes - what helped you decide?

Also happy to help anyone working through the earlier material!
```

### Bad Community Post Example ❌
```
I finished the course. What should I do now?
```
*This won't get responses. Be specific, show what you've done, ask clear questions.*

## Final Thoughts

This assignment is **different** because your career is different from coding exercises.

**There are no right answers**, only answers that are right **for you**.

**The community can guide you**, but only you can make the decision.

**Your career is a product** - build it intentionally.

**Most importantly**: Don't overthink. Choose a direction and START. You can always adjust.
