## Core Assignment — Build Your Own Cross-Chain Bridge

In this assignment, you'll build a complete one-way blockchain bridge that allows users to lock ERC-20 tokens on one chain and mint bridged tokens on another. This will help you understand the fundamental mechanics of how popular bridges like Wormhole, LayerZero, and Multichain work.

Use **Remix IDE** (https://remix.ethereum.org/) or **Foundry** for testing and deployment.

---

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ **Forgetting to approve**: Always call `token.approve(bridge, amount)` before deposit
- ❌ **Not checking allowance**: Verify sufficient allowance before transferFrom
- ❌ **Ignoring return values**: Check return values from transfer functions
- ❌ **Missing events**: Always emit events for off-chain monitoring
- ❌ **No access control**: Protect sensitive functions with onlyOwner or similar
- ❌ **Integer overflow**: Use Solidity 0.8+ for built-in overflow checks
- ❌ **Reentrancy vulnerability**: Follow checks-effects-interactions pattern
- ❌ **No input validation**: Always validate inputs (non-zero amounts, etc.)
