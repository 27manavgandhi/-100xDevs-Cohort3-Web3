# 32.1 - Indexing Solana Accounts

## Topics
- RPC vs Indexing
- Polling vs Webhooks vs Geyser
- Arbitrage systems

## Why Index?
RPC is slow → not suitable for real-time systems.

## Polling
Repeated fetching → high latency.

## Webhooks
Push-based → faster.

## Geyser
Validator-level streaming → lowest latency.

## Architecture
[Stream] → [Processor] → [Trader]
